Get Started

Learn how to use RecuDL in 3 simple steps

1 Configure Authentication

First, you need to set up your authentication credentials to access Recu.me.

Important

You must be logged into Recu.me in your browser to get the required authentication data.

üìã How to get Cookie & User-Agent:

  1. Open recu.me in your browser
  2. Log into your account
  3. Press F12 to open DevTools
  4. Go to Network tab
  5. Refresh the page
  6. Click on any request to recu.me
  7. Find Request Headers section
  8. Copy the Cookie and User-Agent values

‚öôÔ∏è Where to paste them:

  1. Go to the Configuration page
  2. Paste your Cookie in the Cookie field
  3. Paste your User-Agent in the User-Agent field
  4. Click Save Configuration
Go to Configuration

2 Start Downloading

Now you can start downloading videos from Recu.me.

üé¨ Basic Download:

https://recu.me/video/username/play

Just paste the video URL and click "Start Download"

‚è±Ô∏è Time Range Download:

https://recu.me/video/username/play,10:00,25:00,30:00

Download from 10min to 25min of a 30min video

Time Range Format:

URL,START,END,TOTAL

  • START: When to start downloading (e.g., 10:00)
  • END: When to stop downloading (e.g., 25:00)
  • TOTAL: Total video duration (e.g., 30:00)
Go to Dashboard

3 Monitor & Manage

Monitor active downloads, view progress bars, and see real-time statistics

Quick Tips

Multiple Downloads

Paste multiple URLs (one per line) to download several videos at once

Auto-Processing

Videos are automatically converted to MP4 and thumbnails are generated

Resume Downloads

Interrupted downloads can be resumed from where they left off

Real-time Updates
